Templated class to handle the evaluation and storage of one electron integral matrices O, p\+Op, and three p\+Oxp in a finite basis set. 

Templated over storage type (IntsT) to allow for a seamless interface to both real-\/ and complex-\/valued basis sets (e.\+g., GTO and GIAO)
