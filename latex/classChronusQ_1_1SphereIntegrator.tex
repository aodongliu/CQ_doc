\subsubsection*{template$<$class \+\_\+\+QTyp1$>$\newline
class Chronus\+Q\+::\+Sphere\+Integrator$<$ \+\_\+\+QTyp1 $>$}
Sphere Integrator. 

Handles the integration of a 3D (cartesian) function over a ball of a specified radius.

\[
  \int_0^R r^2 \mathrm{d}r \int_0^\pi \sin\pi \mathrm{d}\phi \int_0^{2\pi}\ mathrm{d}\theta
    f(r,\theta,\pi)
\]

Templated over radial quadrature scheme, angular quadrature taken to be Lebedev. Specializes \mbox{\hyperlink{classChronusQ_1_1Integrator2D}{Integrator2D}}.

Coordinate transforms\+:

$ x = r \sin\theta \cos\phi $ $ y = r \sin\theta \sin\phi $ $ z = r \cos\theta $
