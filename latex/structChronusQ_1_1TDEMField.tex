Tensorial specification of an EM field perturbation. 

Handles the specification of various descriptions of the EM field perturbation, such as the multipole expansion.

Expectes field amplidudes of the following\+:

\item Dipole operator -\/ \mbox{[}x, y, z\mbox{]}

Obtain the cartesian field tensor at a specificed time. 

Pure virtual, to be specified by derived classes.

All derived classes are aware of some tensorial representation of the EM field perturbation, $ \hat{v} $. This function returns

\[
  v(t) = \hat{v} \cdot f(t)
\]

where $ f $ is the envelope function.
