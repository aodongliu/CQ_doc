Perform various tensor contractions of the full ERI tensor in core. Wraps other helper functions and provides loop structure.

Currently supports

  • Coulomb-type (34,12) contractions
  • Exchange-type (23,12) contractions

Works with both real and complex matricies

Parameters
[in/out]list Contains information pertinent to the matricies to be contracted with. See TwoBodyContraction for details
