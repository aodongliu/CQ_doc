Detailed Description

template<typename _UnitVector>
struct ChronusQ::TDEMField< _UnitVector >

Tensorial specification of an EM field perturbation.

Handles the specification of various descriptions of the EM field perturbation, such as the multipole expansion.

Expectes field amplidudes of the following:

  1. Dipole operator - [x, y, z]
  2. Quadrupole operator - [xx, xy, xz, yy, yz, zz] ect...
