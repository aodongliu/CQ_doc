template<typename MatsT , typename IntsT >
void ChronusQ::NEOSS< MatsT, IntsT >::computeEnergy ( )
inlinevirtual

Computes the total field free energy of a single slater determinent.

Given a 1PDM and a Fock matrix (specifically the core Hamiltonian and G[D]), compute the energy.

Warning
Assumes that density and Fock matrix have the appropriate form

F(S) = F(A) + F(B) F(Z) = F(A) - F(B) ...

Specialization of Quantum<T>::computeEnergy, populates / overwrites OBEnergy and MBEnergy
