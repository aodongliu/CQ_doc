(a|0) = (P-A)(a-1|0) + 1/2 1/Zeta * N_(a-1)(a-2|0)

(0|0_c|b)^(m) = (P-B)(0|0_c|b-1i)^(m) - (P-C)(0|0_c|b-1i)^(m+1)
